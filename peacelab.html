OVERVIEW

PeaceLab aims at gathering like-minded people who are eager to elevate their state of mind through different mindfulness techniques aside from just meditating.

The MVP focuses on finding a new feature for solving user problem that is not currently included in other meditation apps i.e. "eBook reading" group sessions.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

With today's chaotic and busy lives, increasing prevalence of sedentary lives is prompting individuals to adopt different mindfulness techniques as a stress relieving activity.

Based on my friends' Mental Health startup, I was inspired to design a mindfulness mobile app that could help them in their research phase for the possibility of developing an app.

With the exception of few real market giant competitor like Insight Timer, most of the current already launched mindfulness mobile apps largely focus on the meditation practices.

POSSIBLE SOLUTION

By studying most of the prominent mindfulness market apps including Insight Timer, Calm and Headspace I decided to work on a new feature of “eBook Reading” to my native app design project, building a new competitive advantage.

The “eBook Reading” feature is leaded by a mentor/life-coach for each specific eBook that is offered by PeaceLab.

The aim is to make an online-reading community for users who are enthusiastic in joining a group reading sessions.

Once you join an eBook reading session, you can start discussing your ideas, questions and any related topic in the discussion room.

DESIGN PROCESS

Using “Design Thinking Process” method of UX design, I tried to explore alternative solutions to my problem.

RESEARCH PLAN

Based on Fact.MR The market value for mindfulness meditation apps market is set to be valued at around US$ 180 Million by 2032. The market is forecasted to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% from 2022 - 2032.

COVID-19 induced stress created an inclination towards using mindfulness meditation apps. Physical exercises, breathing techniques and change in daily routine have spurred development of mindfulness mediation apps. Thus, the market will expand to be valued at over US$ 180 Mn by end of 2031.

COMPETATIVE ANALYSIS

What can I learn from others?

I proceeded by conducting a SWOT analysis of two of the most relevant meditation apps that are currently widely used out in the market: Insight Timer and Calm.

OPPORTUNITY

From this round of exploration on most famous meditation apps, I found out that having a visually appealing UI is very important to users. Also by asking from many users of both apps, I found out that most of them prefer using a more minimal and serene interface.

Based on my initial studies, I came of with these main bullet points for starting my design project:

Minimal UI

Professional pictures for courses

“eBook” group reading feature

“Journey” setting feature to stay disciplined
