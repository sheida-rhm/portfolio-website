My Design Journey..

Hello, I'm Sheida, a passionate UI/UX designer coming from the Product Marketing Manager background. I offer a strong skillset in communicating with different cross functional teams, managing stakeholders and performing both quantitative and qualitative consumer research studies.

Optimizing product value and marketing campaigns had always been a big part of my job. It gave me an insight into human decision-making behaviors - a common world with UX design where psychology plays a significant role.

Yet, being so in love and fascinated with the world of graphic design, colors and all the magic that comes with it from long before, I decided to change the trajectory of my career. This new path perfectly positions me to merge my analytical skills with my creative side.

What originally started as learning Adobe Illustrator at the beginning of COVID lockdowns, soon paved my way into the world of UI Design.

I strive to create engaging user experiences that people find inspiring. Learning how to combine fundamentals of interaction design, visual design, wireframing and prototyping techniques has helped me tie together design systems and finding out user-centric solutions of a problem as creatively as possible.

I'm a detail obsessed new technology lover with an eye towards product innovation. So far, I've worked on different case study projects from concept to prototype.

Aside from the world of design and digital arts, one of my greatest passions in life is traveling, working out and above all … SKIING and living in the snowy mountains!

Find me there!

I love meeting new people, so please feel free to reach out.
